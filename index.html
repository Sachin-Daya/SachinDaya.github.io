About Me

I am an Aspiring Electrical and Computer Engineer currently completing a Bachelor of Science in Engineering at the University of Cape Town. My academic journey has equipped me with strong technical foundations across embedded systems, control engineering, communication networks, and digital signal processing, with consistent recognition such as the Dean’s Merit List. I complement this knowledge with hands-on experience gained as an IT Intern at Just ICT, where I worked on network administration, system maintenance, and software development, and as an Electric Motor Technician at Africa Armature Winders, where I developed practical skills in diagnostics, servicing, and precision work on motors and water pumps. Beyond academics and work, I spent over a decade in Scouts South Africa, where I earned the prestigious Springbok Scout Award and honed leadership, problem-solving, and teamwork skills through national and international experiences. These experiences have shaped me into a versatile individual who thrives in both collaborative and independent environments. I am driven by curiosity, creativity, and a commitment to continuous growth. With strong analytical and communication skills, I aim to bridge technical expertise with real-world solutions that have meaningful impact.
